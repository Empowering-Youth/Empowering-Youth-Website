About Empowering Youth

Empowering Youth is a registered non-profit organisation (NPO: 242-292) based in Pietermaritzburg. We are committed to empowering young people from disadvantaged communities by providing access to education, skills development, health information, leadership support, and opportunities for personal growth.

We are committed to empowering young people from disadvantaged communities by providing access to education, skills development, health information, leadership support, and opportunities for personal growth.

Who We Serve

Rooted in the realities of semi-rural, rural, and urban communities surrounding Pietermaritzburg, we work directly with youth who face daily challenges such as poverty, unemployment, gender-based violence, substance abuse, and limited access to quality education. Empowering Youth exists to address these inequalities, offering programmes that build resilience, confidence, and long-term opportunities for young people.

Our Vision

Empowering Youth envisions, that the youth the youth of Umgungundlovu Disctrict can take control of their own lives and make informed decisions that enhance sustainability and well being.

Our Mission

To deliver high-impact, youth-centred programmes that promote education, leadership development, health awareness, personal empowerment, and social justice—enabling young people to become active contributors in their communities and the broader society.

Our Core Values

Develop - Connect - Support

  • Integrity: We act with honesty, responsibility, and transparency.
  • Youth-Centred Development: Young people are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Inclusivity: We create spaces where all young people feel valued and supported.
  • Social Justice: We promote fairness, equity, and human rights in all our work.
  • Community Participation: We work hand-in-hand with communities to identify needs and create solutions.
  • Accountability: We commit to responsible leadership, ethical governance, and efficient resource management.

Our Journey

Community Context

Empowering Youth serves communities grappling with high unemployment, poverty, crime, drug abuse, and limited access to essential services. Many households have young people who lack role models, guidance, or safe spaces to learn and grow. By providing mentorship, training, and support, we help bridge the gap between young people’s potential and their opportunities.

Leadership & Background

Empowering Youth emerged from Source of Hope, a community-driven movement founded on the belief that local leadership can transform communities. Our team includes passionate activists and experienced community developers, committed to walking alongside young people as they shape their futures.

Who we are

Empowering Youth is a registered non-profit organisation (NPO: 242-292) based in Pietermaritzburg. We empower young people from semi-rural, rural and urban communities with education support, life skills, health information and opportunities to lead.

Rooted in the realities of the uMgungundlovu District, we walk alongside youth facing unemployment, poverty, gender-based violence and limited access to opportunities—helping them make informed decisions and build sustainable futures.

Read more about us

Our vision

A generation of young people in the uMgungundlovu District who can take control of their own lives and make informed decisions that enhance sustainability and wellbeing.

Our approach

We combine school-based programmes, community outreach and one-on-one support to connect young people to information, role models and real opportunities.

Develop - Connect - Support

Everything we do is guided by these three pillars, ensuring youth are not just informed but equipped and accompanied.

Community rooted

Born from the Source of Hope movement and led by local activists and community developers.
